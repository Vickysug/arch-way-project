Angelic Reiki (The Hub) is a new intervention, introduced in March 2023. This branch of The Hub project has become a vital part of what we do here; our guests look forward to their treatments every week and the practitioner is fully booked up until the end of the year.

Words from Sarah, our reiki healer:

"Reiki is a hands on (or hands off if the client is not able to be touched) energy healing method, where pure Divine light energy is channeled through me into the client. The gentle touch in itself is therapeutic. I work with Angelic Reiki so the light comes through the Angelic realms and Angels, or higher beings of Spirit are called to assist with the healing. Clients always report feeling more relaxed after a treatment, feeling lighter, bathed in love, and often sensing peaceful presence in the room. The healing can work on a physical, mental, emotional or spiritual level and post- treatment clients have had many and varied positive changes occurring in their lives, such as- sleeping better at night, improved mood, better physical health and finding they no longer want to drink alcohol to excess."
