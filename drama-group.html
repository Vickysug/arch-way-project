Drama Group


Drama group is a new intervention that started in January 2023 and has been hugely successful. The group is led by Josh, one of our project workers who helps to build confidence and allows our guests the opportunity to showcase their creativity. The drama group have performed at a couple of our Open Minds Night events and at our AGM which was very well received by those who attended.
